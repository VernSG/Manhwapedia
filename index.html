Kim Dokja

Kim Dokja is the main protagonist of Omniscient Reader's Viewpoint.

Kim Dokja has a thin face and pale skin. His hair is thin and black; he has long eyelashes and dark circles.[4] His countenance is calm and fresh, although somewhat edgy. There is a marked difference in his appearance when he smiles and when he's expressionless. As an adult, he has a slender physique, being rather skinny prior to the beginning of the scenarios, although he has become rather muscular due to raising his Strength stat.[5] He has burn scarring/tanning due to the probability storm he weathered after Gigantomachia.[6] He also has scars on his hands[7] and presumably the rest of his body as well from the numerous fights he has been through. The number 6424 is "stitched" into his left forearm, from his brief period masquerading as a Removal Slave in the 73rd Demon Realm.[8]

Park Hyung-seok

Kemampuan "Berpindah Tubuh": Hyung Suk memiliki kemampuan untuk berpindah tubuh antara dua penampilan yang berbeda. Satu tubuhnya adalah tubuh yang gemuk dan tidak menarik, sementara yang lainnya adalah tubuh yang sangat tampan dan atletis yang disebut sebagai "Daniel Park.

Kemampuan Bertarung: Seiring perkembangan cerita, Hyung Suk juga mengembangkan keterampilan bertarungnya di bawah identitas Daniel Park, membuatnya lebih handal dalam menghadapi konflik dan pertarungan.

Eugene Lionheart

Eugene Lionheart is a brash, crass, rude and blunt fellow who speaks what is on his mind simply and plainly.[12][13] This was best seen by how he casually flipped a middle finger at the portrait of Sienna the Wise with a grin and beating his adoptive brother Eward for attempting to learn black magic. This is even picked up on in the old fairytales, that describe Hamel as a troublemaker with a fiery personality. Because of that trait, he would frequently end up quarreling with his comrades.He was also power-hungry and ambitious as he was envious of Vermouth's natural talents and strength. At the same time he is quite pragmatical, as he utilizes every resource at his disposal to improve his own power. On the other hand, Eugene is a very prideful individual. He was annoyed by being called 'Stupid Hamel' in the fairytale and despises being patronized.

Contributed by Syachalista

Lloyd Frontera

manhwa berjudul 'The Greatest Estate Developer.' Alurnya memiliki keunikannya sendiri. Cerita ini menceritakan Kim Suho yang sedang asyik membaca novel fantasi dan akhirnya tertidur dengan nyenyak.Setelah terbangun, tanpa disadari, ia mendapati dirinya berada dalam dunia yang menjadi latar belakang novel yang baru saja dibacanya. Sebagai mahasiswa teknik sipil, Kim Suho mengalami kejutan besar karena menemukan dirinya berada dalam tubuh karakter bernama Lloyd Frontera, seorang bangsawan.Meskipun karakter Lloyd Frontera memiliki kekurangan, seperti sifat pemalas dan menjadi biang masalah, serta keluarganya yang terperangkap dalam hutang. Kim Suho tidak tinggal diam menghadapi situasi tersebut.Dengan memanfaatkan pengetahuannya dalam bidang teknik sipil, Suho mulai merancang inovasi yang dapat diterapkan. Ia membuat penemuan yang diharapkan bisa membantunya melewati segala kesulitan yang mungkin dihadapinya di masa depan.
